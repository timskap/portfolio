2GIS Immersive
3D encyclopedia of landmarks for Apple Vision Pro. SwiftUI, RealityKit, Reality Composer Pro, VisionOS.

Created in just 8 days, this immersive app showcases 3D models of landmarks and destinations in spatial computing. Built for 2ГИС (2GIS), one of Russia's leading mapping and navigation companies.

Highlights:

  • One of the first VisionOS apps worldwide (top 600) and the first VisionOS app from Russia
  • Built with SwiftUI and RealityKit for spatial 3D experiences
  • 3D models viewable in AR with detailed information panels
  • Featured by Apple for early Vision Pro support
  • Developed using VisionOS simulator before device launch
